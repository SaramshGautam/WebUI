Signs and Symptoms:

  1. Headache - which does not subside from over the counter medications

  2. Weakness in the limbs, face, or one side of the body

  3. Impaired coordination

  4. Difficulty while walking

  5. Difficulty in routine activities like reading and talking

  6. Noticeable changes in senses like taste and smell

  7. Nausea or vomiting

  8. Memory loss
